Our Story

From spaces to sanctuaries

Esha Interiors was born from a simple belief: every home has a story, and every space has the potential to be beautiful, functional, and deeply personal.

Founded in 2016, we started with a single apartment project in Hyderabad. What began as a passion for transforming living spaces has evolved into a full-service interior design studio that has touched the lives of over 200 families across India. We believe that great design is invisible—it works so seamlessly that you don't notice it; you just feel better.

Our team consists of experienced designers, architects, and project managers who are deeply passionate about what they do. We approach every project as a collaborative journey, listening carefully to your needs, understanding your lifestyle, and translating your dreams into concrete design solutions.

Our Philosophy

We operate on three core principles that guide every decision we make:

  • Listen: We take time to understand your vision, budget, lifestyle, and preferences. Your voice matters most.
  • Design: We craft thoughtful, innovative designs that are both beautiful and practical. Form follows function, and function serves beauty.
  • Transform: We bring designs to life with precision, quality, and attention to every detail. The result is a space you'll love for years to come.

Why choose Esha?

  • Experience: 8+ years of designing homes across India, from apartments to villas, from ₹20K to ₹50L+ projects.
  • Expertise: Specialized knowledge in space planning, materials, color theory, and sustainable design.
  • Quality: We partner with trusted vendors and suppliers to ensure the highest quality finishes and materials.
  • Flexibility: We work with any budget. Whether you want a full home design or a single room makeover, we're here for you.
  • Support: From consultation to completion and beyond, we're with you every step of the way.

Meet Esha

Esha is the founder and lead designer at Esha Interiors. With a degree in Interior Design and over 8 years of experience, she has a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating spaces that tell stories. Her design philosophy is grounded in understanding people—their habits, their dreams, and their values—and translating that understanding into beautiful, functional spaces.

"I believe every home deserves to be beautiful, and every person deserves to feel proud of their space. My job is to make that happen."

— Esha, Founder & Lead Designer
